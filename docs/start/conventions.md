# Procedure Format

The procedures in this manual are presented in a clear, step-by-step format.

- The illustration shows what to do and where to do it

- The task heading tells what to do

- The detailed text explains how to perform the task and provides additional information such as specifications and warnings

Example:

Task Heading: What to Do
## 1.  CHECK PISTON STROKE OF OVERDRIVE BRAKE
(a) Place the SST and a dial indicator onto the overdrive brake piston as shown in the illustration.

SST 09350-30020 (09350-06120)

Set part No.

Component part No.

(b)

Measure the stroke by applying and releasing compressed air
(392–785 kPa, 4–8 kgf/cm² or 57–114 psi) as shown in the illustration.

Piston stroke: 1.40–1.70 mm (0.0551–0.0669 in.)

This format provides the experienced technician with a FAST TRACK to the information needed.

The uppercase task heading can be read at a glance when necessary, while the text below provides detailed instructions. Important specifications and warnings always stand out in bold type.

1. References

References have been kept to a minimum.
However, when they are required, the appropriate page number is provided for reference.

7. Specifications

Specifications are presented in bold type throughout the text where required.

You never need to leave the procedure to look up specifications.
They are also listed in the Service Specifications section for quick reference.

8. Cautions, Notices, Hints

CAUTIONS
Presented in bold type and indicate the possibility of injury to you or other people.

!!! note > "NOTICES"

Presented in bold type and indicate the possibility of damage to the components being repaired.

HINTS
Separated from the main text and not shown in bold.
They provide additional information to help perform the repair efficiently.

9. SI Unit

The units used in this manual are primarily expressed according to the SI Unit (International System of Units).

Alternate expressions are provided in:

The metric system

The English system

Example

Torque: 30 N·m (310 kgf·cm, 22 ft·lbf)
