# Scope & Models

This documentation is a structured, web-based adaptation of Toyota
Service Repair Manual RM790E and related supplements for the 1KZ-TE
diesel engine.

It is intended as a technical reference for inspection, diagnosis,
service, and repair of the 1KZ-TE engine and its supporting systems.

## Covered Engine

**1KZ-TE**
- Configuration: Inline 4-cylinder
- Displacement: 2982 cc
- Induction: Turbocharged
- Injection: Indirect injection (prechamber)
- Fuel system: Electronically controlled Denso VE rotary pump
- Timing drive: Timing belt

## What This Manual Covers

- Engine mechanical systems
- Fuel injection and electronic diesel control (ECD)
- Turbocharging and emissions systems
- Cooling, lubrication, and starting systems
- Factory diagnostic and test procedures

## What This Manual Does Not Cover

- Vehicle chassis, suspension, or body systems
- Automatic transmission internals
- Market-specific wiring outside engine-related circuits
- Non-factory performance modifications
